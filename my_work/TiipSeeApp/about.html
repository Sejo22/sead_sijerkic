About TipSee

TheTipSee™ app is a unique Australian development undertaken by AppSar Pty Ltd and is currently being tested exclusively in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

The aim of the app is to return the art of tipping back to that of being a choice for the consumer to make, completely independently of the eventual final service charge.

As we move away from the use of cash as a means by which to advance incremental reward, the basis on which we’re being asked to advance a tip - as part of the final digital payment experience - leaves many accepting the additional charge against their emotional beliefs with a sense of having been coerced into making the payment for fear of being considered ungrateful.

TipSee™ merely seeks to readdress the balance and return tipping to the way it used to be. The way it should be. The old fashioned way.

That being, an independent and emotional choice based on the delivery of exceptional service and not an enforced incremental charge made when the consumer least expects it.
