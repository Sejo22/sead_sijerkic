Frequently asked questions...and answers!

A few questions and answers that address the obvious:

Is the TipSee™ app free to install?

The TipSee™ app is free to install.

What does it cost to use the TipSee™ app?

Just $0.10 is deducted from the total value of a tip being offered by a tipper to a tippee.

Why try and change the way consumers tip?

We’re trying to retain the old fashioned way of tipping with the TipSeeTM app because we believe the change from tipping with cash to tipping via a digital payment experience has been exploited in such a manner as to force consumers to tip.

Wouldn't a tippee get more if I just add a tip when I pay the bill?

Merchants pay a fee for accepting card payments and we hope that the eventual fee paid when using the TipSee™ app will be less than that paid by the merchant for the same value and as a result, the tippee will receive more.

Why can't I see a record of who I've tipped on the TipSee app?

Because the philosophy of the TipSee™ app is to re-introduce tipping the old fashioned way. An emotional reward made at a given point in time for a particular service that then needs no retrospective consideration. Just like tipping with cash.

When will the TipSee app be available thoughout Australia?

Once we’ve completed our testing in Victoria we aim to market the TipSee™ app throughout Australia and encourage Australians to tip the old fashioned way. The right way!

What do I do if I have a question that is't answered here?

Please feel free to email us at Contact@tipseeapp.com and we’ll do our best to answer your question.
